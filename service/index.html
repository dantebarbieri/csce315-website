The Big Event

Texas A&M students organize an annual volunteer event called The Big Event. I helped clean fallen branches and limbs from people's yards and also to clean backyards of leaves and dirt that inhibited the drainage of water. It was a muddy job, but I'd gladly do it again!

Hurricane Harvey Relief

In the aftermath of Hurricane Harvey, my family and I were lucky enough to escape unscathed from flood waters, however, we know people who weren't so fortunate. I helped my family to assist in the tearing down of drywall and removal of furniture that had soaked in floodwater.

Anadarko Shred Day

Anadarko Petroleum Corporation was an energy company in Texas until recently and they used to organize a "Shred Day" where employees of Anadarko and their family members could bring large boxes of papers and also electronics that they wanted shredded. Anadarko would rent a shred truck company that specializes in handling that kind of recycling and I volunteered for a couple of years to help people unload their stuff and to prepare it for shredding.
